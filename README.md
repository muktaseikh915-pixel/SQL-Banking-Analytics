# 🏦 Banking Analytics SQL Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This Banking Analytics project leverages SQL to analyze customer, account, loan, and card data in order to generate actionable business insights. The project focuses on customer segmentation, credit risk evaluation, deposit analysis, customer value assessment, and executive-level KPI reporting.

The goal is to demonstrate how SQL can be used to transform raw banking data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Segment customers based on account balances and overall relationship value.
- Identify high-value customers for premium banking services.
- Evaluate loan portfolio risk using customer credit scores.
- Analyze deposit distribution and customer profitability.
- Measure card product adoption across customers.
- Compare regional banking performance.
- Create executive-level KPI reports for business monitoring.

---

## 📊 Key Analyses Performed

### 1. Customer Segmentation
Categorized customers into:
- VIP
- Premium
- Regular
- Basic

based on account balances.

### 2. Above-Average Balance Analysis
Identified customers whose balances exceed the overall bank average.

### 3. Customer Deposit Analysis
Calculated total balances held across multiple customer accounts.

### 4. Customer Ranking
Ranked customers using advanced SQL window functions.

### 5. Decile Analysis
Divided customers into ten groups based on account balances.

### 6. Deposit Growth Tracking
Calculated cumulative deposit growth using running totals.

### 7. Credit Score Analysis
Segmented customers into:
- Excellent
- Good
- Fair
- Poor

credit categories.

### 8. Loan Risk Assessment
Classified borrowers into:
- Low Risk
- Medium Risk
- High Risk

using credit score thresholds.

### 9. Geographic Performance Analysis
Analyzed:
- Top cities by total deposits
- Average credit score by city

### 10. Customer Value Segmentation
Grouped customers into:
- Elite
- High Value
- Medium Value
- Low Value

based on total relationship value.

### 11. Executive KPI Dashboard
Generated banking KPIs including:
- Total Customers
- Total Accounts
- Total Loans
- Total Deposits
- Average Credit Score
